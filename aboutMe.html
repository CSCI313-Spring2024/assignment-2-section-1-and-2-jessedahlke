About Me

Background

Growing up in a rural town in central Minnesota certainly isn't the usual start for those interested in and pursuring computer science professionally. However, I've had interest in the topic for as long as I can remember and hope to take that experience into the workforce after I graduate.
Outside of the tech world, some of the things I enjoy the most are kayaking, pickleball, and watching football. A larger list of my favorite hobbies, movies, and websites are listed below. Kayaking is one of my favorite pastimes due to its tranquility and the ability it has to remove so much stress of everyday life.
My work experiences includes retail in my high school years and working the production line last summer. During high school, I worked at the same grocery store for three years. During my time there, I was promoted to a shift manager position in which I would manage a group of employees and close the store each night. At my short summer job on the production line at 3M I assessed issues with automated machines and communicated with a small team to ensure the product was made accurately and efficiently. Although I enjoyed my time at my previous workplaces, as I go through college I continue to aim my vocational goals towards more of the things that interest me.
